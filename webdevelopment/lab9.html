DAVV

Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, formerly University of Indore, is a state university located in Indore, India. It was named after Ahilya Bai Holkar, the 18th century Queen and ruler of Indore, which was a part of the Maratha Empire. DAVV is the only university in Madhya Pradesh accredited A+ Grade by NAAC. Wikipedia DAVV is famous for its infrastructure and its various faculty members. Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya has a big campus with a well stocked library.
